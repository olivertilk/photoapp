Story number and name: 5 - Deleting Comments, Photos, and Users
Story points: 4
Brief description to see the story in action: To delete a comment, click the “Delete comment” text link at the bottom of each comment made by the logged in user. Story number and name: 6 - Photo like votes
Story points: 4
Brief description to see the story in action: On the Photos page, below each photo is a “Like” or “Unlike button, depending on whether the photo has been liked. When a photo is liked a “Like thumb” image appears on the right side below the photo to indicate it has been liked. In addition, there is a like count of the photo next to the Like button that is updated when it is liked or unliked. The photos are displayed in sorted order first by the number of likes and second by the timestamp.
